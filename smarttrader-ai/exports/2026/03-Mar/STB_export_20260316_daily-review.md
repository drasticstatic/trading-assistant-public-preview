## 🔑 Key Lessons

1. **SL is a pre-entry decision.** The only valid mid-trade SL change is if the entry thesis is explicitly invalidated by a rule defined *before* entry — not a structural observation discovered in the moment. "Aligning with FCR ray" is not a pre-entry SL rule.

2. **Trade 2 is the template.** SFP wick to entry, engulf confirmation, multi-instrument cross-check, ZTH + FVG structure, conservative TP with structural logic, no touch after entry. That's the execution standard.

3. **Conservative TP near session close is correct.** OB + FVG cluster at target, approaching RTH close, ZTH KEY level overhead — that's enough structural logic to collect. Fishing for more with 20 minutes of RTH left is not the move.

4. **RTY is a valid primary.** Gave the cleanest read today. Full engulf on RTY when NQ/ES/YM were near-only. Use the cleanest instrument, not necessarily the usual one.
