## 🔗 YM's Role in the SMT Trio

YM is the **third leg** of the NQ / ES / YM tripod.

**When YM diverges from NQ:**
YM is Dow-heavy (financials, industrials, healthcare).
If a tech selloff hits NQ hard but YM holds up,
that's sector rotation / defensive positioning —
not a broader market reversal.

**When to use YM for the FCR entry:**
If NQ and ES are unclear/choppy at the open but YM
has a clean 15-min first candle structure, YM can
be the entry instrument. The SMT confirmation still
applies — verify all three are directionally aligned.

**YM's unique edge today:**
The pre-annotated FVG makes the entry zone more
visible on YM than on NQ or ES. If the FCR setup
triggers on all three, YM's FVG zone gives the
most defined limit entry price range.
