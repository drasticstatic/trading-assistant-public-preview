## ⚡ Why CL Is Analyzed Independently

Crude Oil and Natural Gas are **not** a standard SMT
divergence pair. While both are energy commodities,
their price drivers are fundamentally different:

- **CL** is driven by: OPEC production decisions,
  geopolitical risk (Middle East, Russia), global
  demand data (IEA/EIA weekly inventory), USD strength
- **NG** is driven by: domestic storage levels (EIA
  Thursday), weather (heating/cooling demand), LNG
  exports, production data

These idiosyncratic drivers mean CL and NG frequently
move independently — even in opposite directions on
the same session. CL is analyzed on its own structure.
