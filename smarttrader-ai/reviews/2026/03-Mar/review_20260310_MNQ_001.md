## 🧠 Behavioral Notes

**What went right:**
- Entry planning: A+ process. Structured, multi-layer confluence, patient execution.
- SL discipline: Held despite 1-tick approach and active temptation. Pattern 7 fix confirmed live.
- Bug discovery: Caught a significant indicator error during a live session without it affecting the trade materially.

**What needs work:**
- **Exit passivity (Pattern 8):** Two manual exit opportunities recognized and not taken. No active decision made. Prop firm made the final exit. An entry process this strong deserves an exit process to match.
  - At ~14:30: held unrealized gains, felt closing would "hurt" a potential winner. Missing: a partial profit rule or a clear "take some off here" level.
  - At ~16:45: near break-even, nervous, held hoping to recover. Missing: a scratch rule ("if I'm back to break-even after a deep reversal, this trade is done").
- **No personal exit before prop firm close:** Christopher had already noted a preference to be out by 4 PM. The trade ran 42 minutes past that. The prop firm's hard close should never be the exit mechanism — this is an account protection boundary, not a trade management tool.

**Emotional state:** Exhausted by 16:59. "Nervous" at 16:45. Wanted to move SL at ~16:09 — chose correctly not to. Recognized both missed exits in real time. Self-awareness is high; the next step is converting that awareness into action at the moment.
