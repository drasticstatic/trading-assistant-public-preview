## 📖 Session Narrative

> Pre-market plan: [premarket_20260324_summary.md](https://github.com/drasticstatic/trading-assistant/blob/main/smarttrader-ai/analysis/premarket/2026/03-Mar/premarket_20260324_summary.md)

The sell limit at 6673.0 was originally conceived the night of March 23, set after the 02:30 ET London STB coaching call, during which price had been making an overnight recovery from the ETH lows. The equal highs zone from the overnight recovery, combined with the manipulation wicks at that level, created the structural entry thesis. A prayer was said before rest. The bracket was in place.

Through the full March 24 RTH session — STB snapshot neutral-bullish/strong-bullish, 9:30 FCR spike bullish across all four indices, morning spent in Scenario C with YM/RTY diverging bullish against NQ/ES weakness — the sell limit sat untouched above the market. SMT lower highs formed on NQ/ES/YM through midday. IT OG sell signal appeared on the 5-min at noon; IT Pro+ did not confirm. Multiple times the question arose: move the entry lower? Each time the answer was no — the entry was at structural resistance, the impulse to move it was Pattern 7 energy applied to the entry instead of the SL.

Christopher rested again in the afternoon. Intentionally. Bracket confirmed. Prayer said. He returned to find the fill.

The position filled at 16:26 ET (ETH session) — over 36 hours after the original thesis was formed. It moved immediately to a MAE of 4.75 pts adverse, recovered, reached +33 pts MFE at 16:40 ET, and AutoLiq closed it at 16:59 ET at +24.5 pts. The TP at 6588.75 (+84.25 pts, ~$4,212) was not reached.

The old object tree entry at 6880.5 — a projected resistance from an earlier analysis — was wicked to exactly during the overnight manipulation. The market found every level that had been identified. The structural patience to enter at 6673.0 rather than 6880.5 captured a more confirmable, less aggressive entry with defined risk.

The SL was placed and not touched throughout. Pattern 7 had its cleanest pass of the recovery arc on the day with the highest deadline pressure.

**What is the work: Pattern 8.**
Exit via AutoLiq. MFE +$1,650 at 16:40 ET. Actual exit +$1,225 at 16:59 ET. The difference: $425 left relative to best exit; $2,987 left relative to full TP. Zero active exit decisions across all trades in the recovery arc continues. AutoLiq is an Apex constraint, but the pattern is consistent: the exit is never in Christopher's hands. **The old entry note.**
