## 🧠 Behavioral Arc — The Patterns That Built This

### Pattern 7 — Stop Loss Modification
**Status as of April 2026:** Active but partially contained.

First documented: Pre-Feb (unnamed). Named formally after Mar 3. Recurred in 3 of 4 March Week 3 trades. Each manifestation found a different rationalization: structural alignment, noise survival, post-fill protection removal. The circuit-breaker (name the rationalization before touching the SL) partially works. The rationalized form still gets through.

### Pattern 8 — Exit Passivity
**Status as of April 2026:** Active. Zero active exits made across any trade in the recovery arc.

Named Mar 10. Every exit in the documented arc has been via SL hit, resting TP, or AutoLiq. No active mid-trade exit decision has ever been made. The fix identified: write exit rules (partial TP + scratch condition) before placing any bracket.

### Pattern 9 — Pre-Rest Order Hygiene
**Status as of April 2026:** Named and under watch.

Confirmed Mar 20: old bracket filled while resting, SL then canceled, position held through -$925 MAE before AutoLiq. Rule: cancel ALL orders before stepping away from desk.
