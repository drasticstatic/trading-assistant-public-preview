## 🧠 Behavioral Notes

- **Binary options** — structurally negative expected value for retail traders (house edge built in through asymmetric payout: win 80-85%, lose 100%). The -$151.62 net loss across 741 trades reflects this structural disadvantage, not necessarily poor execution.
- **No behavioral patterns to classify** — binary options don't have SL, TP, or exit management in the traditional sense. Each trade is a fixed bet.
- This appears to have been an exploratory phase, not a serious trading approach.
- Activity stopped (or was not exported) after Dec 2025. No 2026 trades in the export.
