## Key Patterns Identified

### Pattern 1 — FOMO Entry (Feb 23)
```
Trigger:    Correct directional read → impatience → B-grade level entry
Symptom:    Filled at 3/5 (mitigated) instead of 5/5 (active)
            SL placed AT the 5/5 → swept by 1 tick
Result:     Stop hit on a correct trade → full TP reached after
Fix:        FVG must align with 5/5 (active) for A+ grade
Status:     ✅ Fixed on Feb 25 (5-layer confluence, 5/5 alignment)
```

### Pattern 2 — Wrong Scenario (Feb 24)
```
Trigger:    Anxiety + greedy → counter-trend long on bearish day
Symptom:    EMAs steeply red dominant = explicit Scenario B veto
            Entered anyway → 66 seconds → full SL
Fix:        IT Foundation EMA gate: red dominant = no counter-trend long. Period.
Status:     ✅ Fixed on Feb 25 (EMAs green dominant = Scenario B valid)
```

### Pattern 3 — Coach-Lag (Feb 24)
```
Trigger:    Following STB/ZTH entries late at the open → wrong timing
Symptom:    Entry in wrong direction after coach trigger = bad fill
Fix:        Pre-market preparation → independent entry → coaches for confluence
Status:     ✅ Reversed on Feb 25 (in trade before ZTH's 25,348 level reached)
```

### Pattern 4 — Stop Movement (Feb 13)
```
Trigger:    Emotional pressure → moved stops → accounts blown
Symptom:    SL moved repeatedly under adverse price action
Fix:        SL is placed structurally and never moved. Period.
Status:     ✅ Held on Feb 23, Feb 24, Feb 25, Feb 26 — habit confirmed
```

### Pattern 5 — Level Abandonment Under Pressure (Feb 26)
```
Trigger:    Time pressure (needed to get to work) + FOMO of missing fill
            → adjusted entry from structural level (25,060) to round
              number (25,000) to "guarantee" the fill
Symptom:    Entry 60 pts away from structure → same SL (25,093) →
            risk tripled ($198 → $558 for same structural stop) →
            SL hit. At 25,060 with SL above 25,139 (first bounce high):
            SL NOT hit, trade alive, direction confirmed profitable.
Result:     -$558 on a directionally correct trade
Fix:        Pre-plan entry AND SL before session. No last-minute
            adjustments. "If it doesn't fill at the structural level,
            the trade said no." External time pressure is not a reason
            to modify a trading plan.
Status:     ✅ Fix applied Feb 27 — level held under same pressure within 24 hours
Note:       Root cause same as Pattern 1 (FOMO) but mechanism differs:
            Feb 23 = entered at lower-quality FVG level (3/5 vs 5/5)
            Feb 26 = moved entry AWAY from structural level entirely
```
