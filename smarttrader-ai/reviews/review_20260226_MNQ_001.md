## ⚡ What Happened in One Paragraph

Feb 26 opened as a Scenario A SHORT day — FCR confirmed bearish at 9:45, STB coach entered short at 9:15 via order block + SMT divergence (exited at 4.44R), ZTH coach looked for and got a bounce long as predicted. The bounce ran for ~2 hours from the session lows, testing the 38.2%–50% Fibonacci retracement zone off the ETH high. Multiple Inevitrade members including Christopher identified the second retrace setup (1-2-3 continuation pattern) and planned a short re-entry at 25,060 — a structural resistance level on NQ. Under pressure from time constraints (needing to get to work) and fear of missing the fill, Christopher adjusted the entry from 25,060 to 25,000.5 — a round number with no structural basis. 3 MNQ contracts were entered SHORT at 13:37:22 EST. Price moved slightly in favor (MFE: 24,977 — 23.5 pts, ~$141 unrealized) before reversing and triggering the SL at 25,093.5 at 14:47:39 EST for a loss of -$558. After the SL was hit, price continued lower into the ETH session, reaching 24,954 at ~6 PM ET — confirming the directional read was correct. The planned entry at 25,060 with a SL above the first bounce high (25,139) would NOT have been stopped out and would have been profitable. 25,093 is a known strong structural level; it acted as resistance on the second touch and was the precise SL trigger.
