## The Strategy: Stack Accounts As You Earn Them

**Do not open new prop firm accounts before passing the current evaluation.**
Opening accounts before readiness = unnecessary fees + more rules to track + more surface area for mistakes.

---

## Progression Phases

### Phase 1 — Current Focus
**Account:** Apex Trader Funding ($100K Full, Tradovate)
**Strategy:** ZeroToHero (STB/ICT foundation layer)
**Goal:** Pass the evaluation with discipline — 7 minimum trading days, hit profit target, zero rule violations

**Do NOT open Lucid or Tradeify until Apex is passed.**

---

### Phase 2 — After Apex Pass
**Open:** LucidFlex Evaluation

**Why LucidFlex over LucidPro:**
- One-time fee, no monthly rebilling — zero cost pressure to "justify" the account
- No Daily Loss Limit — one less hard rule to manage while Inevitrade SMC execution is still developing
- Scaling plan in funded = earn your way to larger size (discipline feature, not limitation)
- 50% consistency with cushion is more forgiving than LucidPro's 40% payout requirement
- LucidPro's advantages (pass in 1 day, full contracts immediately) are only relevant when already highly confident in execution

**Strategy on Lucid:** Inevitrade / SMC (STB/ICT foundation layer)

---

### Phase 3 — After LucidFlex is Funded and Validated
**Open:** Tradeify Select Challenge

**Why Select Challenge:**
- No consistency requirement removes one variable during the learning phase
- 3-day eval is achievable without open-ended monthly cost pressure
- After passing, choose funded structure based on discipline level at that time:
  - **Select Flex** — if discipline is strong (no DLL, more freedom)
  - **Growth Challenge** — if additional structure/guardrails still helpful (DLL as soft guardrail)

**Strategy on Tradeify:** Inevitrade / SMC (STB/ICT foundation layer)

> ⚠️ Tradeify requires at least **one trade per calendar week** — unique rule to track.
