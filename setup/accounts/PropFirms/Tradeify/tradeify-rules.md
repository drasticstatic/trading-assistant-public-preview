## Prohibited Trading Activities

### 1. Minimum Weekly Activity ⚠️
- **At least one trade per calendar week is required**
- Failure to meet this = potential account restriction

### 2. No Hedging
- Simultaneous opposing positions are completely prohibited

### 3. No Mini + Micro Simultaneously
- Cannot trade both mini AND micro futures contracts at the same time on the same account

### 4. No Microscalping
- 50% or more of account profit must come from trades held **longer than 10 seconds**
- Scalping under 10 seconds for the majority of profits = violation

### 5. No Automated/HFT Bots
- Bots allowed only if you own it exclusively and it is not shared across multiple firms
- No high-frequency trading bots
- No collaborative/team trading — each trader executes independently

### 6. No Manipulative Trades
- No strategies exploiting platform errors
- No disruptive trading practices
- No external/delayed data feeds
- No pre-arranged or non-competitive trades

### 7. Consistency Rule
- Must maintain consistent trade size, stops, and targets
- Avoid maximum-size single trades that dominate account profit

## Payout Requirements (General)

- **Profit split:** 90% trader / 10% Tradeify
- Processing: within 24–48 hours (72 hours for after-hours requests)
- Account reconciliation: daily between 5:00–6:00 PM or 6:00–8:00 PM EST
- Minimum payout: varies by account type ($500–$1,500)

### Tradeify Elite Live (after 5 payouts or $80K combined payouts)
- Starting balance up to $20,000
- Must complete 20 trading days with $200+ daily profit before withdrawal eligibility
- Dynamic daily loss limits adjust based on prior day's ending balance
- Minimum payout: $250

## Claude's Enforcement Checklist (Tradeify)

When reviewing any Tradeify trade, flag if:
- [ ] Confirm account type (Growth Challenge / Lightning / Select Challenge / Select Flex / Select Daily)
- [ ] **Weekly trade requirement met** — at least 1 trade this calendar week
- [ ] Position size within current account limits
- [ ] EOD drawdown not approached (flag if within 20% of limit)
- [ ] DLL not hit or approached (where applicable)
- [ ] Consistency rule check — single day not dominating total profit
- [ ] No mini + micro contracts traded simultaneously
- [ ] No trades held under 10 seconds making up majority of profit
- [ ] Trade held past **4:00 PM ET** (personal EOD target)
- [ ] Emotional state suggests revenge trading or forced entry
- [ ] Inevitrade SMC strategy applied correctly (bias, rehearsal, multiple limits)
